About Us

Entrepreneurship Development Cell is the only independent cell of the institute commited to establishing an entrepreneurial environment in the campus. EDC organises events round the year, ranging from awareness programmes, workshops, E Talks by renowned entrepreneurs and most importantly E Summit, which is said to be the second largest E Summit of eastern India. We are infact only one of the three NITs to have collaborated with the PM Yuva Scheme. We at EDC firmly believe that Entrepreneurship is the key to India's development and with our varied initiatives, we intend to promote potential startups from our college and help India become the Innovation Hub of the world.
